INTRODUCTION

Today, there are about 50 million horses in the world and 200 million people are interested in or own horses. The horse industry only had sales in Sweden of just over SEK 46 billion in Sweden last year. The horse industry is a huge partner with great business potential. We develop and market the innovation Horse Metro, which is an internationally cohesive platform for goods and services within equestrian sports, something that until now has not previously existed. Imagine a social platform similar to Facebook with blog opportunities where you, as either a private person or a company, can sell and buy locally but can easily find goods, services, horses and stallions for breeding internationally. We want to gather all the horse enthusiasts, goods and service providers in one place to facilitate social and business networking and simplify the goods and service exchanges within equestrian sports. Devgarden builds a large international company and is looking for users, companies and partners who want to be visible on our platform.
